Pilot License

Upon completion of training and issuance of Private Pilot License (PPL), a student can fly solo or carry passengers in visual flight conditions. The PPL allows the holder to fly for recreational purposes or on flights for non-commercial purposes.

Pilot License with Instrument Rating & Multi Engine Rating

A Commercial Pilot License with a current Instrument Rating allows the holder to be employed as a pilot in organizations conducting commercial operations such as airlines, air charter organizations, etc. or as flight instructors at a flight school.
